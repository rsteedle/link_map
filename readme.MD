# link-map

## Household income and Link light rail expansion map

This map shows the expansion of [Sound Transit](https://www.soundtransit.org/)'s Link light rail, from the first segment opening in 2009 through today, as well as how the socioeconomic profile--as measured by median household income--of the neighborhoods it serves has changed over that time. 

### Data & methods
Median household income by Census tract is from [2010-2024 ACS 5-year estimates](https://data.census.gov/table?q=S1903:+Median+Income+in+the+Past+12+Months+(in+2024+Inflation-Adjusted+Dollars)&g=040XX00US53,53$1400000). Note, the data presented on the map for 2025 and 2026 uses 2024 ACS data, and for 2009 uses 2010 ACS data.

Census tracts are defined as "served" by Link if any part of their geometry falls within 1/2 mile of a Link station. The average median income of "served" tracts is calculated as the unweighted mean of the median household incomes of all tracts served by Link in that year.
